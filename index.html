Crystal Collector

How to play

The game will display a random number.

There are five crystals. Each crystal is worth a random amount.

The values of the crystals are hiden from you. Add up 4 crystals to get to the total of the random number

WIN: If the four crystals add up to the random number, you win!

LOSE: If the total of the four crystals add up to more than the random number or if adds up to less than the random number, you lose!
