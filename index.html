Crystal Collector

How to play

You will be given a random number.

There are four crystals. Each crystal is worth a random amount.

The values of the crystal are hiden from you. Add up the crystals to get to the total of the random number

WIN: If you can add up enough crystals to get the total of the random number, you win!

LOSE: careful! You only have 5 tries and if you add too much to your total and it is more than the random number, you lose!
